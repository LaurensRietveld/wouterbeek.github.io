Versioning information is stored as queryable metadata

Every dataset that is upload and each subsequent edit that is made to it is stored in the Version Management software Git. All versioning metadata (author, time, version(s) derived from) is stored as RDF metadata. This means that the customer can query the entire provenance trail of her data warehouse.
