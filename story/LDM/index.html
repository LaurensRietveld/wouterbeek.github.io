Class hierarchy

The class hierarchy of Wikidata's theorem concept (wde:Q65943) has multiple subsclasses. Most subclasses have no instances in DBpedia, but they may have instances elsewhere, e.g., in the Wikidata endpoint.

Subjects hierarchy
