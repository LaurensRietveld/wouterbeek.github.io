How did we do it?

(1/3) LOD Laundromat

Beek & Rietveld et al. 2014, LOD laundromat: a uniform way of publishing other people's dirty data

(2/3) Header Dictionary Triples (HDT)

(3/3) Linked Data Fragments (LDF)
