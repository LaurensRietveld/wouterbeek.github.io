Anyone Can Say Anything About Anything

Triply is your Linked Data Store in the Cloud. Triply allows you to store, combine, and query your own Linked Data, and integrate it with other datasets. Triply also allows you to publish your Linked Data for others to query and reuse through standardized APIs.

Anyone Can Publish Anything About Anything

Scalable
Whether you have 1 or 1 billion triples, Triply Cloud can handle datasets of any size.
Available
With Triply your Linked Data endpoints and APIs are guaranteed to have high availability. Due to failover, your endpoints and APIs will also work under intensive data use.
Flexible
With Triply you can combine multiple datasets into one. You can also import other datasets as context for your data. This allows you to query across multiple datasets in an integrated way.

Anyone Can Ask Anything about Anything

Interoperable
Triply implements open standards like RDF and SPARQL that are maintained by the Internet community. This ensures that your data is interoperable with other tools, and with the rest of the Internet.
Multi-paradigm
You can query data with the powerful SPARQL query language, but also with easy-to-use JSON/REST APIs. Triply also has client support for popular programming languages.
Linked Data IDE
Triply comes with a Linked Data IDE. Features like syntax highlighting, auto-completion, and direct query feedback allow you and year team to be more productive when working with Linked Data.

Anyone can Share Anything with Anybody

Collaborative
Triply allows you to create organizations where multiple team members can work on the same datasets together.
Searcheable
Triply includes data catalog features that allow you to efficiently search through your data collection. With Triply, your team members are always able to find the data they are looking for.
Secure
Not all data is intended to be published out in the open. With Triply you decide whether a dataset is published as Open Data, or whether it is only accessible to your team or to you personally.

On-site deployments with Triply In-House

You don't like to store your data in the Cloud? We also offer Triply In-House, a product that can be deployed on-site. With Triply In-House your data never leaves your premises.
