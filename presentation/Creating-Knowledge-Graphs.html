Syntax of names

  • IRI: <https://demo.triply.cc/academy/pokemon/id/pokemon/mew>
    • Datatype IRI: <http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#integer>
  • Literal: "40"^^<http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#integer>
    • Language-tagged string: "ミュウ"@ja
